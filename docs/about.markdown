## About Kanilrós

Kanilrós is the artist name of @hrafnkellbrimar. The name "Kanilrós" means "Cinnamon Rose," and the music spans varying styles but is mainly centered around the electric guitar.

### Musical Style

The work encompasses:
- **Instrumental compositions** featuring electric guitar as the primary instrument
- **Multi-instrumental arrangements** including mandolins, acoustic guitars, and bass
- **Electronic soundscapes** blending digital instruments with traditional sounds
- **Vocal tracks** (beginning with the second album)
- **Genre influences** ranging from rock and folk to funk, disco, and experimental music
