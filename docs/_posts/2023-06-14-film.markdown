*Film* is the instrumental debut by Kanilrós. Released on 15 April 2023, the 18-track album introduced the project through music centered on electric guitar, supported by mandolins, bass, acoustic guitar, and electronic textures.

### The beginning of Kanilrós

As the first Kanilrós release, *Film* established the project's independent approach: the music was written, performed, recorded, mixed, and produced by Hrafnkell Brimar Hallmundsson. With no vocals, the instruments carry the melodies and give each piece its own shape.

### Sound and instrumentation

Electric guitar is the main voice across the record, but the arrangements also draw on acoustic strings and electronic sounds. That combination created the broad instrumental palette that would continue to develop on later Kanilrós albums.
