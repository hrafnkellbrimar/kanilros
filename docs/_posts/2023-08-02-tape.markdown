*Tape* is the third Kanilrós album: ten guitar-led rock songs released on 2 August 2023 during the project's prolific first year.

### The third album

Arriving less than four months after the debut *Film*, *Tape* completed a run of three Kanilrós albums in 2023. It continued the move into vocal, song-based material begun on *Hive* while keeping electric guitar at the center of the music.
