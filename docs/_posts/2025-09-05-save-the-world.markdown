Released on 5 September 2025, *Save the World* is the fifth Kanilrós album. Its eleven tracks take different shapes and sizes, with every song placed deliberately within the complete record.

### A complete record

Variety is central to *Save the World*, but the album was designed to work as one continuous sequence rather than a collection of unrelated songs. Its running order gives each track a particular role within the larger record.

### Independent production

As with the earlier Kanilrós releases, the album was written, performed, recorded, mixed, and produced independently. It continues the project's genre-fluid approach while presenting the songs as a deliberately ordered whole.
