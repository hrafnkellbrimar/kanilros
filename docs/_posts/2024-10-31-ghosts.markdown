Released on Halloween 2024, *Ghosts* is the nine-track fourth Kanilrós album. Its songs explore hidden people, vampires, friendship, memory, and the afterlife.

### Stories of the seen and unseen

The record gathers supernatural imagery and human relationships into the same world. Songs including “Little Birds” and “Afterlife” approach that world from different directions, connecting the mysterious and imaginary with friendship, memory, and loss.

### Making the album

*Ghosts* followed the three albums released during the project's first year and began a new chapter for Kanilrós. “Little Birds” also became an official music video before appearing on the completed album.
