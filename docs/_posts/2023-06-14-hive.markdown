*Hive* is the second Kanilrós album and the first to feature vocals. Released on 14 June 2023, the 11-track record followed the instrumental debut *Film* and widened the project's musical vocabulary.

### A new voice

Introducing vocals marked a major change for Kanilrós. The songs retain a guitar-led rock foundation while moving through funk, disco, metal, and folk influences.

### Sound and instrumentation

The arrangements bring together electric guitar, mandolins, acoustic guitars, bass, digital instruments, and vocals. Rather than settling into one genre, *Hive* uses that instrumental range to move between contrasting styles within a single album.
